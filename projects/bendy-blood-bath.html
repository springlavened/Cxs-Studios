PROJECT OVERVIEW

Overview

Bendy and the Blood Bath is a psychological horror Friday Night Funkin' mod centered around trauma, revenge, and redemption. Rather than focusing on monsters alone, the story explores the emotional scars left behind by violence and asks whether hatred can ever truly heal the pain that created it.

ARCHIVE ENTRY

Story

Bendy and the Blood Bath follows a broken Bendy after witnessing Joey Drew intentionally kill Boris during a violent argument with Henry Stein. Seeing his closest friend die before his eyes leaves Bendy traumatized, changing him forever and filling him with overwhelming rage.

Consumed by revenge, Bendy hunts Joey Drew and becomes covered in the same darkness he despises. After finally getting revenge, he realizes that Boris is still gone—and the pain never disappeared.

Boyfriend enters this nightmare to confront the different versions of Bendy's fractured mind, including Pickle Bendy and The Taker. These manifestations represent Bendy's anger, grief, fear, and hatred. The real battle isn't defeating Bendy—it's helping him overcome the monster his trauma is slowly turning him into.

PROJECT HISTORY

History

Development of Bendy and the Blood Bath began in October 2025. Created by SubMan91 with KirbyFanYt as co-owner, the project aims to tell an emotional horror story through original music, unsettling visuals, and psychological storytelling.
