OVERVIEW

About the Project

Bendy and the Blood Bath is a psychological horror Friday Night Funkin' mod that reimagines the world of Bendy through a darker and more emotional story. Rather than focusing solely on horror, the project explores grief, trauma, revenge, and the struggle to overcome inner darkness while delivering original music, redesigned characters, and a unique narrative.

STORY

The Ink Never Healed

Bendy and the Blood Bath follows a broken Bendy after witnessing Joey Drew intentionally kill Boris during a violent argument with Henry Stein. The loss of his closest friend leaves Bendy traumatized, forever changing the innocent cartoon he once was.

Driven by grief and revenge, Bendy hunts down Joey Drew, but in doing so becomes consumed by the very darkness he despises. After finally taking his revenge, he discovers that nothing has changed—Boris is still gone, and the pain remains.

As the nightmare continues, Boyfriend must face Bendy in a desperate attempt to help him confront the trauma that's consuming him. Along the way, he'll battle twisted manifestations such as Pickle Bendy and The Taker, each representing the fear, anger, and hatred growing inside Bendy's fractured mind.

In the end, the greatest enemy isn't Bendy himself—it's the darkness threatening to erase the person he used to be.

PROJECT HISTORY

How It Began

Originally created in October 2025, Bendy and the Blood Bath is one of the earliest horror projects associated with CXS Studio. Since its creation, the project has continued to evolve through new story ideas, redesigned characters, original music, and an expanding vision that aims to deliver a darker and more emotional experience inspired by the world of Bendy.
