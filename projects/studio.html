WHO WE ARE

About CXS Studio

CXS Studio is an independent creative studio focused on creating memorable horror experiences through games, Friday Night Funkin' mods, animation, music, artwork, and original storytelling. Our goal is to bring unique ideas to life through creativity, collaboration, and a passion for building projects that leave a lasting impression on players and fans alike.
