STORY

The Fall Begins

GodsFall is CXS Studio's complete reimagining of the Irida era. Rather than simply retelling the original story, the project expands its world with new lore, redesigned characters, original music, and a stronger horror atmosphere while remaining true to the heart of the original concept.

FEATURES

What Makes GodsFall Different

Redesigned Characters

Returning characters have been rebuilt with new designs while keeping the spirit of the original project.

Original Characters

Meet brand-new faces created exclusively for the GodsFall universe.

Expanded Story

A completely reworked narrative with new lore, mysteries, and darker themes.

Original Music

Custom songs and remixes created specifically for the project.

Horror Atmosphere

A cinematic horror presentation inspired by analog horror and psychological suspense.
